### For Recruiters Viewing Your Project:

1. **Use Windows Game Bar** (Built-in)
   - Press `Win + G` to open
   - Click record button
   - Show the application features
   - Stop recording with `Win + Alt + R`

2. **What to Record:**
   - âœ… Application startup

4. **Save Location:** Videos saved to `C:\Users\[YourName]\Videos\Captures`
